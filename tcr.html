About Thrissur


Known as the cultural hub of Kerala, Thrissur is nestled in the picturesque coastal region of southwest India, steeped in history entwined with the vibrant spice trade. Thrissur holds a special place for nurturing classical Keralan performing arts, sacred landmarks, and the legendary Thrissur Pooram festival, as well as the vibrant celebrations of Onam and the revered Vadakkumnathan and Guruvayoor Temples.



For an exercise in pure tranquility, consider adding an overnight houseboat excursion through the local backwaters to your itinerary. And if the stars align and you find yourself in Thrissur during the grand temple festival of Pooram, you'll be treated to a captivating symphony of live music, dazzling fireworks, enchanting shadow puppetry, and majestic processions featuring resplendently adorned elephants. Visit the breezy beaches and thick forests, witness beautiful waterfalls and amazing people.
